\subsection{Mathematical Formula-ex.3-matrices}
Here are three kinds of matrices below.
%%圆括号矩阵
\[
    A = \begin{pmatrix}
            a_{11} & a_{12} & a_{13}    \\
            0 & a_{22} & a_{23}    \\
            0 & 0 & a_{33}
        \end{pmatrix}_{n\times n}
\]

%%方括号矩阵带矩阵大小
\[
    A = \begin{bmatrix}
            a_{11} & \dots & a_{in} \\
              & \ddots & \vdots  \\
            0 &  & a_{nn}
        \end{bmatrix}_{n\times n}
\]

%%矩阵元素位数不同对称，需要mathtools宏包
\[
    A = \begin{pmatrix*}[r]
            10 & -10 \\ -20 & 3
        \end{pmatrix*}_{2\times 2}
\]
